What We Do

Our Services

Metal Roofing

Durable, energy-efficient metal roofing systems for homes and commercial properties. Standing seam, corrugated, and more — built to handle Kansas weather for decades.

Metal Siding

Low-maintenance metal siding that protects and beautifies. Wide color selection to match your vision, with superior resistance to rot, pests, and fading.

Metal Buildings

Custom metal buildings engineered for your needs — workshops, garages, storage facilities, and commercial structures. Strong, versatile, and cost-effective.

Pole Barns

Agricultural and residential pole barns designed for the Kansas landscape. Ideal for livestock, equipment storage, hay, and more. Built tough, built fast.
